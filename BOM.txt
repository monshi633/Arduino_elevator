#Bill of materials for a 3 floor version

Electrical components:

(1)  - ESP32-VROOM-32D devkit USB-C
(1)  - Power supply and USB-C cable

(3)  - LM393 H2010 photoelectric sensor (one for each floor)

(9)  - Green LED (the amount of floors for each floor)
(3)  - Yellow LED (one for each floor)
(12) - 220 ohm resistor (one for each LED light)

(3)  - Pushbutton (one for each floor)
(3)  - 10K ohm resistor (one for each pushbutton)

(2)  - 10K ohm potentiometer
(2)  - 25V 100uF capacitor

(1)  - 9V DC brushless motor
(1)  - L293D or L298N H-bridge motor driver

(1)  - 9V battery (for the DC motor)
(1)  - Power switch

Other hardware:

()  - Prototype PCB
()  - 18AWG Wire (different colors)
()  - Heat shrink tubing
()  - Soldering
()  - Male/female pin connectors
